About

Dungeons & Dragons
Character Generator

Using Javascript and JQuery, we created a program that does the heavy lifting in creating a player character for D&D 5th Edition. Users simply choose a race and class, roll ability scores and choose their equipment. The program will print out your character's stats with all appropriate adjustments and ability score modifiers, attack bonuses and damage, etc.
